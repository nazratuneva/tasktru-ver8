Community Engagement At Your Fingertips

Driven by a seamless design and intuitive dashboard, you’ll be up and running in minutes.

tasktru allows you to manage your community responsibilities and safety needs with optimal flexibility, through one single platform of engagement.

tasktru is pre-configured to provide all the features you need to meet the demands of your community with superior efficiency. We understand that communities have unique needs, and that residents within your community have high expectations for convenience, service, and most importantly, safety. This is where tasktru comes into play, bringing management of operational demands and safety measures within your community into one fully integrated application. With tasktru, you can achieve a smooth and hassle-free operation, ensuring that your residents, suppliers, and guests feel valued, safe, and secure, with automated security measures and immediate attention to concerns or queries. You can automate and control access to your community, assign responsibilities to your community suppliers, and process community dues seamlessly for resident convenience. Your residents can also self-engage any service offering you have set up on your community platform with a simple click, including requests for maintenance or activation of safety features. tasktru empowers your residents to work together to create a safe and welcoming community environment for all.

Product Features

Access Management

Community access control has never been easier. Users, residents, and guests are able to apply uniquely generated access codes right from their phones, for entry or exit to the premises at any time of day or night.

Service & Supplier Management

Allocate work orders to your community suppliers with streamlined efficiency. You’ll get real-time updates and accurate information on progress, to ensure your projects stay on track and on budget.

Payment Management

Set up your accepted payment types, and allow residents to initiate payments directly upon setup. Additionally, you can choose to apply penalty rules for overdue outstanding debts, and can configure the app so that automated services or features will be denied to debtors.

Seamless & Convenient Invoicing

All payments are invoiced within the app, and all financial records are stored in the app history to settle disputes or address user questions. Outstanding payments are also recorded to track account actions and payment progress.

Communication Management

Publish general announcements and distribute digital communications to all residents, to ensure the community is aware of urgent information or updates. You can communicate directly with individual residents, send a group message to the entire community, or distribute internal communications without the need to coordinate multiple platforms.

Onboarding Of Residents, Staff Members & Suppliers

Onboarding of new users and workers often requires extensive customization that is time consuming and resource intensive. This has been expedited with an automated process which allows you to easily register residents and staff members to get onboard the platform. Once their accounts have been created, they can start utilizing all the features that are pre-configured for their engagement needs and enhanced community value.

Self-Service & Fulfillment

Residents are able to post a community request on demand, and can track progress and confirm completion according to integrated workflow.

Reports & Charts

Get insights to all community management operations, work fulfillment, and task performance in real time.

Information Box - See Something, Do Something

Allows users to quickly and easily record an observation, along with supporting evidence, to enhance the safety of the community. Residents are able to become active participants in the security of their communities by making suggestions that positively impact the community and promote meaningful dialogue among community members.

Polls

Make your decision-making process modern, secure, and effective by allowing your users to seamlessly participate in your published polls/surveys and view results instantly. Participants can vote from anywhere using their smart devices, ensuring they never miss out on an important chance to voice their feedback.

Emergency Alarm

Provides users the ability to notify the community of an emergency or immediate life-threatening danger nearby, with a simple push alarm button to initiate an emergency alert. An automated help request will be sent within seconds to notify appropriate authorities, thereby improving emergency response time, ensuring resident safety, and resolving emergency situations as fast as possible when time is of the essence.

Emergency Contact

Give your users access to necessary contact details by setting up clear and accurate contact information for different needs and community management sources.

Get Started

As a resident of a community, you can set up your account in minutes and start engaging from your portfolio. Here’s how:

1. Create or Login to your tasktru account.

To view or manage your engagements, you’ll need to be logged in to your tasktru account. Don’t have one? You can create a personal account here for free.

2. Go to your portfolio, click Options, and add your community

Select Create/Add to edit your portfolio, then add your community to begin engaging through tasktru. Please note that in order for you to connect and become an active member of the platform, your community admin will need to pre-register you for approved use.

3. You’re all set up!

Now that you’re up and running, start your engagement actions or manage your existing tasks, responsibilities, or requests.

And you can even share the good news with everyone immediately. The platform will send an alert to friends and other community members to facilitate fast personal connections and meaningful engagements.

As a supplier to a community, you can set up your account in minutes and start engaging from your dashboard. Here’s how:

1. Create or Login to your tasktru account.

To process engagement requests for your community, you’ll need to be logged in to your tasktru account. Don’t have one? You can create a business account here for free, specifically for this purpose. Only staff members/suppliers of communities using the Business-Plus account can use this account to process requests.

2. Add your organization name to become active.

The community admin will need to have pre-registered you in order for you to connect and become an active member of the platform.

3. Go to your Dashboard for actions.

See all requests that have been automatically assigned to you and start processing them instantly. It’s very easy and interactive to process assigned requests with our intuitive dashboard interface.

As a community Administrator / Leader, you can set up your account in minutes to start managing your community operational demands and safety measures. Here’s how:

1. Create or Login to your tasktru account.

As a community admin, you’ll need to be logged in to your tasktru account to manage community responsibilities, resident interactions, and supplier engagements. Don’t have one? You can create a Business-Plus account here.

2. Subscribe to your desired product from the Options menu.

Subscribe to your desired product type and set up your community functions.

3. You’re all set up!

Now that you’re up and running, start getting insights for community enhancement and other actions to make your community a safe and welcoming place.
